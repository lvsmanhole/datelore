Today in history

On May 31
  1. 1911

    The Titanic is launched

    The hull of the RMS Titanic slides into the River Lagan at Harland & Wolff in Belfast, before more than 100,000 spectators.

    Maritime history
  2. 1889

    The Johnstown Flood

    The South Fork Dam fails after days of heavy rain, sending 20 million tons of water into Johnstown, Pennsylvania.

    Disaster
  3. 1859

    Big Ben begins keeping time

    The Great Clock of Westminster starts ticking above the Houses of Parliament in London — its chimes have marked the hour ever since.

    London
